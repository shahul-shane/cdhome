About us

Cones and Dreams is a brand new ice cream parlor located in Horamavu, Bangalore. We opened our doors in June 2023, bringing a fresh and delightful experience to the ice cream scene in the area. Despite being a new venture, our team at Cones and Dreams is committed to crafting exceptional ice cream treats and providing a memorable experience for our customers.

Our Passion for Ice Cream:


At Cones and Dreams, we are passionate about creating the most delicious and indulgent ice cream treats. Although our team may be new to the industry, we have put in countless hours of research, experimenting, and taste-testing to ensure that our ice creams are of the highest quality. We believe that passion and dedication can overcome any lack of experience, and our commitment to excellence shines through in every scoop we serve.

Our Irresistible Softies:


One of our specialties at Cones and Dreams is our soft-serve ice cream, also known as "softies." Our softies are incredibly creamy, velvety, and bursting with flavors. Despite our limited experience, we have perfected the art of creating softies that rival those made by experienced professionals. Whether you prefer classic flavors like vanilla and chocolate or more adventurous options like strawberry cheesecake or salted caramel, we have a softie that will satisfy your taste buds.

Thick Shakes and More:


In addition to our delightful softies, we also offer a wide range of thick shakes and other frozen treats. Our thick shakes are made using premium ice cream blended with various flavors and topped with whipped cream and delicious toppings. You can choose from our menu of classic shakes or create your own unique combination.

Creating Our Own Waffle Cones:


To enhance your ice cream experience, we make our own waffle cones in-house. The aroma of freshly baked cones wafts through our parlor, inviting you to indulge in the perfect combination of crispy and creamy. Our waffle cones are handcrafted with care and add an extra element of joy to every scoop of ice cream.


Come and join us at Cones and Dreams, where every scoop is a step towards making memories that will last a lifetime.
